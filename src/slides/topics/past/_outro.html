The past was a hostile "wild west" frontier

We survived endless clearfix rituals, tables disguised as layouts, sprites stitched by hand, and hacks that broke with every new browser build. The result was fragile experiences for users and sleepless nights for developers.

Today we celebrate that those painful tricks belong to history, and we never want to go back.

Remind the audience that Baseline did not exist at the time, so support checks meant guesswork and constant firefighting.
